Comments

The provided code contains two examples:
 - HDR Super-resolution: reproduce similar results than Figure 11 in the paper
 - light field factorization: implement the nonnegative light field factorization (Eq.10 in the paper) further described in supplementary materials. 
These two examples work out of the box and produce similar results than those found in the paper. 
However they cover only a subset of the proposed method, and specific hardware would be required for fully reproduce this work.
